Membership

There are currently over 50 members in the Band who perform regularly in the local area at our formal concerts, charity events and free summer performances in the town.

We are always looking for new players to join the Band and we welcome players of all ages of a reasonable standard (above grade 3 for a guidance).

Rehearsals are held on Monday evenings at Withycombe Raleigh Primary School from 7pm - 9.30pm.
